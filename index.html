Lyceum of Aparri

CSO

Central Student Organization

Welcome to the Central Student Organization portal. Here you can check your attendance records, view upcoming events, and stay connected with all campus activities. The CSO works to enhance student life and represent student interests across all departments.

College of CICS

Innovating for the Future

The CICS department offers cutting-edge programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, and Information Systems. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with the technical skills and innovative mindset needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

College of Commerce

Shaping Business Leaders

The Commerce department offers comprehensive programs in Business Administration, Accountancy, and Entrepreneurship. We develop future business leaders through rigorous academic training, practical experiences, and ethical business practices.

College of Engineering

The Builders of the Future

Our Engineering programs provide students with strong technical foundations and problem-solving skills. Through hands-on projects and industry partnerships, we prepare engineers who can design, innovate, and implement solutions for complex challenges.

College of Health

Caring for Community Wellness

Our Health Sciences programs prepare students for careers in nursing, medical technology, and public health. With state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty, we develop healthcare professionals committed to serving communities with compassion and expertise.

College of Liberal Arts

Exploring Human Expression

The Liberal Arts department offers programs in Communication, Psychology, and Political Science. We foster critical thinking, effective communication, and cultural awareness to prepare students for diverse careers and meaningful civic engagement.

College of Criminal Justice

The Criminology program prepares students for careers in law enforcement, corrections, and security administration. Our curriculum emphasizes ethical practice, forensic science, and criminal justice system operations to develop competent professionals.

College of Education

Shaping Future Educators

Our Education programs prepare students to become effective teachers and educational leaders. Through rigorous coursework and practical teaching experiences, we develop educators who can inspire learning and make a positive impact in diverse educational settings.

K-12 Education

Foundation of Lifelong Learning

Our Basic Education program provides a strong academic foundation for elementary and high school students. We foster holistic development through a balanced curriculum that emphasizes academic excellence, character formation, and essential life skills.
