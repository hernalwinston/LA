About Lyceum of Aparri

Our Story

Founded on July 5, 1967 by Archbishop Teodulfo S. Domingo, Lyceum of Aparri began as a response to community needs in coastal towns. Starting with just secondary education, Liberal Arts, and Business Administration, the institution has grown into Northern Luzon's premier Catholic educational center.

Through visionary leadership under figures like Msgr. Diosdado A. Talamayan and Rev. Fr. Joel M. Reyes, the school expanded significantly. Key milestones include establishing the Lyceum of Aparri Hospital in 1980, achieving Level II Accreditation status in 1988, and continuously adding diverse programs from Engineering and Nursing to Computer Science and Maritime studies.

Today, Lyceum of Aparri stands as the largest diocesan-run learning institution in Northern Luzon, recognized among the Top Ten schools in Region 02. It maintains memberships in prestigious educational associations including CEAP, PAGE, and PACU, continuing its mission of providing quality Catholic education while adapting to evolving community needs.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that fosters academic excellence, moral integrity, and social responsibility. We are committed to developing individuals who will contribute positively to their communities and the nation.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, innovative programs, and graduates who make significant contributions to society through their knowledge, skills, and values.

Expected Lyceum Graduates Attributes

Effectively expresses ideas both in written and spoken form, listens with understanding, and interacts confidently in diverse situations.

Integrity

Demonstrates honesty, fairness, and moral responsibility in all academic, personal, and professional endeavors.

Critical Thinking

Analyzes information carefully, solves problems logically, and makes sound decisions based on evidence and reasoning.

Care for Spiritual Life

Shows faith, compassion, and respect for others by living out positive values that strengthen both personal and community well-being.
